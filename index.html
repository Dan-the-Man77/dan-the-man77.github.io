WHO WE ARE

Making the most of every learning moment, from day 1 ...

We’ve been shaping, fueling and nurturing children’s natural curiosity for over 15 years now. We’re driven—through our passion for early childhood education and community commitment—to prepare children for school and for life. We’re here to educate and encourage your child to do more and be more—not just while they’re with us, but outside the classroom!

We believe curiosity and learning generate their own momentum, and we’re dedicated to developing what’s unique in every child we care for!

OUR SERVICES

Our focus...

Child Development Curriculum
Our child development based curriculum is derived from different educational establishments to suit each stage of child development. It consists of a comprehensive collection of activities designed to meet the needs of every child in our care.
Technology Education
As the world becomes increasingly reliant on technology, computer literacy has become as essential as reading/writing. We use the latest technology to make learning engaging and fun.
Health & Fitness
We teach children to make healthy food, fitness, and hygiene choices. Nutritious meals and outdoor playground time are part of each day at Child Growth Services.
Character Essentials ℠
It's crucial for children to learn the values that will help them develop into well-rounded citizens. Character Essentials ℠ uses character education to derive learning from each interaction.
